Frequently Asked Questions

Can I create a personal account without a school? +

No. EduSystem follows a "school-centered" approach. Only schools can register first, then create accounts for their users (teachers, parents, students). This ensures data security and proper accountability.

What if I'm a teacher at multiple schools? +

You will have separate accounts for each school. Each school owns your account for their institution. You can use the same email/phone, but they will be distinct accounts managed by each school.

How do parents get access to the system? +

Schools create parent accounts when they register students. Parents receive their login credentials via SMS or email from the school. Parents cannot self-register - they must be added by their child's school.

What information do I need to register my school? +

You need: School name, address, contact information, TIN number, and details of the school administrator who will become the Super Admin. The registration process takes about 10 minutes.

Is there a free trial? +

Yes! Schools get a 30-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required for the trial period.
